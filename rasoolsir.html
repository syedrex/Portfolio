About Me

I'm SHAIK RASOOL With over a decade of experience in Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, and IoT, I've been on quite a journey. Along the way, I've published 18 international papers and secured eight patents, all while diving deep into the fascinating world of tech.

Teaching has been a fulfilling aspect of my career too. From unraveling the intricacies of Java to exploring the possibilities of Python, I've had the privilege of guiding students through these transformative subjects. In between research and teaching, I've found time to contribute chapters to academic books, delving into topics like AI in healthcare and safeguarding IoT networks.

Attending workshops and seminars has been a cornerstone of my professional growth, with over 110 workshops and 250 seminars/webinars under my belt. It's been an enriching experience, connecting with fellow enthusiasts and staying ahead of the curve.

I'm truly grateful for the recognition I've received from industry leaders like IBM and SmartInternz for my contributions to Data Analytics and AI. It's validation of the passion and dedication I bring to my work every day.
